# Pacific Health Medical Centers — Telehealth & Logistics HQ
## Virus Treatment Centers (VirusTC) • Operational Repository

Welcome to the centralized governance and tracking repository for the **VirusTC Telehealth and Prescription Logistics Infrastructure**, headquartered at **Yesler Towers (Seattle, WA)**. 

Operating under the clinical umbrella of **Pacific Health Medical Centers**, this repository hosts the data validation frameworks, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and encrypted logistical manifests required to manage a high-volume, compliant mail-order pharmaceutical and investigational product pipeline supplying verified hospital networks nationwide.

---

## Headquarter Profile: Yesler Towers

Our centralized facility at Yesler Towers serves as a non-dispensing, digital-first medical command center.

*   **Clinical Staff Structure:** 100% Telehealth Physicians and Licensed Cross-State Consultative Attenders.
*   **Core Operational Mandate:** Remote patient triage, Institutional Review Board (IRB) protocol coordination, prescription authentication, and cold-chain supply chain oversight.
*   **Regulatory Footprint:** No raw pharmaceutical compounding or physical patient traffic occurs on-site at Yesler Towers. All physical fulfillment is routed through validated, food-grade/medical-grade agricultural production farms and certified compounding distribution hubs.

### 1. Telehealth Prescription Authentication
*   Physicians conduct comprehensive remote clinical assessments, cross-referencing diagnostic data with master protocol parameters.
*   Prescriptions are digitally signed, encrypted, and transmitted directly to production partners along with required FDA Expanded Access (Compassionate Use) authorization keys.

### 2. Prescription Logistics & Delivery Assurance
*   **Cold-Chain Integrity:** All shipments utilize real-time cellular data-logging sensors to record internal storage container temperatures continuously from dock deployment to hospital receipt.
*   **Chain of Custody:** High-priority lots undergo multi-factor signature verification upon delivery at hospital receiving bays, ensuring zero gap in accountability.
*   **Quality Reconciliation:** Receiving departments upload verified physical logs directly back to our automated database to match against master manufacturing lot balances.
